About Us

Welcome to Empowering the Nation, a visionary initiative founded by Precious Radebe. Our mission is to empower and uplift domestic workers and gardeners in our community through comprehensive skills training.

Our Story

Born out of a personal passion to make a difference, our institution was inspired by Precious's own family history. Her parents and elderly relatives were denied the opportunity to access formal education and upskill themselves. This sparked a desire to create a platform that would provide similar individuals with the chance to acquire new skills, enhance their employability, and increase their earning potential.

Our Purpose

At ETN, we bridge the skills gap by equipping domestic workers and gardeners with marketable skills, enabling them to secure better-paying jobs or establish their own entrepreneurial ventures. Our training programs cater to the needs of both employees seeking to upskill and employers looking to enhance the expertise of their staff.

Our Vision

We envision a community where domestic workers and gardeners are valued, empowered, and equipped to reach their full potential. By providing accessible training and skills development, we aim to foster financial stability, independence, and a brighter future for our students and their families.

"Cultivateing excellence, one trade at a time."

Precious Radebe
