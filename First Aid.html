Certificate in First Aid

This comprehensive 6-month Certificate in First Aid program equips students with the practical skills and knowledge required to succeed in the hospitality and health industry. Students will learn the fundamentals of first aid awareness, basic life support and emergency life saving procedures.

Duration

6 Months

Fees

R1500


COURSE CONTENT

1. Wounds and bleeding.

2. Burns and fractures.

3. Cardio-Pulmonary Resuscitation (CPR).

4. Emergency scene management.

5. Respiratory distress e.g., Choking, blocked airway.
